About
The Base Schooling System Ezzi City

Established in March 2018 as the Les Anges Montessori Academy for toddlers, ZAF has since grown into a global network of private schools, and institutes, imparting distinctive and meaningful education to students from pre-school to midle school. The Base Schooling System Ezzi City , a project of ZAF, has completed more than sixteen years of service to educational development across the country with remarkable success. Established in 2002, it now has 900+ campuses & the market leader in Pakistan's educational landscape with a presence in over 225+ cities and a student strength exceeding 190,000+. This makes The Educators outreach the largest compared to any other private school in the world.
